ExonViz

A web-application to visualize transcripts and pathogenic variants along these transcripts to identify mutational hotspots and exons eligible for exon skipping..

Open ExonViz Open Pre-Print

Genetic Therapy Generator Toolkit

A web-application that automatically generates all possible exon skips for a specified transcript and determines the predicted effect on the protein, allowing for streamlined assessment of exon skipping approaches.

Open GTGT

TReXomeDB

A comprehensive database incorporating all exons that contain tandem repeat protein domains to identify novel exon skipping targets missed by conventional exon skipping approaches.

Open TReXomeDB

N1C Gene Registry

The N1C Gene Registry catalogs ongoing and developed N=1 therapies, approved genetic therapies, and pathogenic variants assessed for genetic therapies, providing a comprehensive resource to support international collaborations in precision medicine.

Dutch Center for RNA Therapeutics

Dutch Center for RNA Therapeutics (DCRT) is a nonprofit consortium of the medical centers Leiden University Medical Center (LUMC), Radboudumc (Nijmegen) and Erasmus MC (Rotterdam). The aim is to develop tailor-made RNA therapy for patients with rare genetic disorders.
